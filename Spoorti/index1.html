10 Business Ideas

1. Smart Fish Market Auction Platform

The Smart Fish Market Auction Platform is a digital system that connects fishermen and buyers through real-time online bidding. Fishermen upload their daily catch with base prices, and buyers place bids from anywhere. The platform automatically selects the highest bidder for each fish lot, ensuring transparent and fair pricing. By reducing middlemen and speeding up the selling process, this system helps fishermen earn more and allows buyers to access fresh seafood quickly and efficiently.

SDG 8 – Decent Work and Economic Growth

2. Eco-Tourism Route Optimization App

The Eco-Tourism Route Optimization App helps tourists explore the best attractions through smart, personalized route planning. Users select their interests such as beaches, waterfalls, temples, or nature trails, and the app calculates the most efficient travel path using real-time data. This ensures a smooth and time-saving travel experience while promoting sustainable and well-managed tourism.

SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities

3. Smart Irrigation & Paddy Water Distribution System

The Smart Irrigation and Paddy Water Distribution system helps farmers manage water efficiently using sensor data and intelligent allocation algorithms. It continuously monitors soil moisture, weather conditions, and crop growth stages, and automatically adjusts irrigation schedules to save water and improve crop productivity.

SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption and Production

4. Autonomous Beach Cleaning Robots

Autonomous robots patrol beaches to detect waste using sensors and collect it efficiently through smart path-planning algorithms. These robots reduce the need for manual labor, maintain cleaner beaches, and support sustainable coastal and marine ecosystem management.

SDG 14 – Life Below Water

5. Micro Solar Energy Trading Platform

The micro solar energy trading platform enables households to buy and sell surplus solar power. Smart matching and pricing algorithms efficiently connect buyers and sellers, helping reduce energy costs, increase renewable energy usage, and promote sustainable energy adoption.

SDG 7 – Affordable and Clean Energy

6. Fishery Fuel Optimization System

The fishery fuel optimization system identifies efficient routes for fishing boats using path-finding algorithms and historical catch pattern data. It minimizes fuel consumption, increases profits for fishermen, and promotes environmentally responsible and sustainable fishing practices.

SDG 14 – Life Below Water

7. Smart Farmer Price Forecasting

The smart farmer price forecasting system uses historical market data, seasonal trends, and forecasting algorithms to predict crop prices. It helps farmers choose the best time to sell their produce, reduce losses, and improve income stability.

SDG 2 – Zero Hunger

8. Water Taxi & Coastal Boat Routing

The water taxi and coastal boat routing system provides fast and efficient water-based transportation across rivers and coastal regions. Smart routing algorithms reduce travel time, fuel costs, and improve connectivity while supporting sustainable urban transport.

SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities
