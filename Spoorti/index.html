Smart Fish Market Auction Platform

The Smart Fish Market Auction Platform is a digital marketplace that connects fishermen and buyers through a real-time auction system. Fishermen upload their catch details, and buyers place bids remotely. The system ensures transparent pricing, quick transactions, and increased earnings for fishermen.

Algorithms & Data Structures Used

1. Fish Listing & Upload

  • Hashing: Used for authentication and fast ID lookups (O(1)).
  • AVL / Red-Black Trees: Store fish listings in sorted order with fast insert/search (O(log n)).

2. Live Bidding System

  • Max Heap / Priority Queue: Track highest bid efficiently (Insert: O(log n), Max: O(1)).
  • Merge Sort: Sort bid histories or fish lots (O(n log n)).

3. Winner Selection

  • Heap pop(): Extract the highest valid bid (O(log n)).
  • Dijkstra / Radix Trees: Fast search through filtered bid data.
  • KMP / Rabin–Karp: Pattern matching for searching listings.

4. Delivery & Scheduling

  • Greedy Assignment: Assign delivery agents efficiently.
  • Graph-based Routing: Optimize delivery paths using shortest-path algorithms.

Eco-Tourism Route Optimization App

The Eco-Tourism Route Optimization App helps travelers, guides, and tourism agencies discover the most sustainable, scenic, and efficient travel routes. It analyzes environmental impact, travel time, terrain difficulty, biodiversity zones, and user preferences to recommend eco-friendly routes. The system supports hikers, nature tourists, and wildlife explorers by optimizing paths while ensuring minimal ecological disturbance.

Algorithms & Data Structures Used

1. Eco-Route Mapping & Storage

  • Geo-Hashing: Converts location coordinates into compact hashes.
  • Quad Trees: Store and query geographical regions efficiently.

2. Route Optimization Engine

  • Dijkstra / A* Algorithm: Compute optimal routes.
  • Dynamic Programming: Reduce carbon footprint.
  • Merge Sort: Sort routes by difficulty or rating.

3. Wildlife-Safe Routing

  • K-Means Clustering: Group wildlife zones.
  • KMP / Rabin-Karp: Fast trail searching.

4. Travel Scheduling

  • Greedy Time-Slot Allocation: Assign visits efficiently.
  • Graph-Based Weather Routing: Adjust paths dynamically.
